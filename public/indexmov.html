Five random good movies
you might want to watch
before you turn thirty

(I just thought of them rn)

Here are a few of my favorite movies
that appeared to have stayed with me.
I believe every young adult would benefit
from watching these.

1. No Country for Old Men

This chilling portrayal of a ruthless
mass murderer will make you question
how and who you trust in the "real" world.

2. Spirited Away

In contrast, this animated masterpiece will
take you on a ride of childlike adventure and wonder,
reminding us to nurture the child within us.

3. Into the Wild

Nowadays, we tend to be increasingly
disconnected to our natural surroundings.
This film encouraged me to seek a simple life and
reconnect to what really matters while pursuing my
dreams and freedom.

4. The Departed

One of my favorite crime films of all time,
the comprehensive quality of this masterpiece and
its storytelling will have you on the edge of your seat.
Also, I love Leonardo.

5. The Hunt

This list wouldn't be complete without
a European director and one of my
favorite European actors. One of the most gripping and
unbelievably believable dramas you'll ever watch.
